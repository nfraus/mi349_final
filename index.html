Spartasoft Studio

Spartasoft Studio is a Registered Student Organization at Michigan State University founded in the spring of 2022. Spartasoft Studio is a game studio run entirely by students, in which the group members are split into teams based on discipline, which collaborate on a Unity project in a single semester.

Tomb for Two

Tomb for Two is a co-op dungeon delver, where friends fight enemies and solve puzzles to escape the dungeon. I collaborated with 65 people on this project, working from my team of 12 programmers. I developed the enemy behaviors, the room handling completion and resetting, puzzle interactions with the rooms and objects in the room, player attacks, parts of the health system, and bug fixes. I acted as a leader to my team by helping other programmers with their code, I guided programmers through development of enemy subclasses and weapon attacks, and coordinated with other disciplines and their directors.

Knight by Night

Knight by Knight is a singleplayer story-adventure game, fighting off enemies to save the townspeople from their bad dreams. In this project I collaborated on a Unity project of 30 people, within a team of 10 programmers. I programmed the enemy behaviors, the health system, and fixed bugs nearing our release.
